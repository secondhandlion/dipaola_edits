Honor/Conduct

MIDN LTJG Diorio/MIDN ENS Kolodziejczak


Integrity and decorum are paramount qualities of a Naval Officer. The Honor and Conduct Officers of 30th Company endeavor to educate each midshipman in Thirty on the importance of these qualities. These distinguished and capable first class midshipmen also serve as representatives for midshipmen who are being tried for honor or conduct offenses, ensuring that justice and judgment are dealt fairly.
